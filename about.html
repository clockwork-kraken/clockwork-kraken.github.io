Meet the team behind Clockwork Kraken

'Clockwork Kraken' is the name of the small studio we've built and are building, based out of Hamilton, Ontario. Our goals are to craft fun and enjoyable experiences and to - one day - pull the medium in new directions. Right now, we are working on 'The Dungeon Crew', a real-time RPG game that explores the commericialisation of dungeoneering for Android and iOS.
