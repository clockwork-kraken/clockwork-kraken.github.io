August 10, 2017

The Dungeon Crew: Headed to B-B-B-B-!

We're going! That's right, your friendly neighbourhood Clockwork Krakens will be at the CNE! At BitBazaar! August 25 to August 27! We plan to show off some exciting new features of the game, a boss battle and more! Do swing by!


Moreover, we'll be selling some merchandise, so if you want some swag, you know where to grab some--more details coming soon. We'll also be holding some special events on Saturday: our composer (heyo, Brian! ) will be selling EPs with the game's music (with custom artwork by our artist, Oscar) as well as his own original music; there'll be a tournament!

Details coming soon!

The (Not So) Big Con Bravo Wrap-up Post

A massive thank you to the organisers of Con Bravo for having us! It goes without saying we were thrilled with the response and all the feedback we received been crucial in helping us make the game better--so thank you, one and all, for giving our game a go!
