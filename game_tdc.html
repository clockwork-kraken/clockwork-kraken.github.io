The Dungeon Crew is an Android/iOS game that marries the traditional tropes of real-time tactical RPGs to the casual approach of mobile gaming. The game takes place in a traditional fantasy setting where exploring dungeons has become a commercial sport. Adventurers form crews and seek sponsorships for this lucrative sport. You play as the manager of a new crew starting at the bottom: direct the crew within dungeons, enter them into tournaments in the arena, recruit and equip new members, seek patrons and secure sponsorship for various bonuses.
